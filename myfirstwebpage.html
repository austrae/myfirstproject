Audie the Audacious Maine Coon Cat

I have a big, fluffy orange cat named Audie.
His name is Audie because he is audacious, audible, and orange like autumn foliage.
My family adopted him from Standish Humane Society in the late summer of 2015.
For an older cat, he has quite the personality, and he loves to meow.

Audies favourite things are:

  • Water, specifically from the shower
  • People food
  • Sleeping on my head

I love Audie so much because he:

  1. Is an odd character
  2. Meows on cue
  3. Loves me very much
  4. Is a majestic beast
